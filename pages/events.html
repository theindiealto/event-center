events & amenities

a welcoming space for connection, creativity, and community growth.

amenities

Event Hall

Spacious and versatile, perfect for weddings, banquets, performances, and large gatherings. Includes stage, AV setup, and flexible seating.

Meeting Rooms

Modular spaces ideal for classes, meetings, workshops, or private functions. Available in various sizes to meet your needs. Contact us to book.

Library

A quiet, welcoming space with books for all ages, free Wi–Fi, comfortable reading nooks, and computer access. Great for studying, researching, or unwinding with a good story.

Gymnasium

Full–size indoor court perfect for basketball, volleyball, pickleball, and community sports leagues. Also available for open play, fitness programs, and special events.

Pool

Heated indoor pool offering lap swim, family swim times, water aerobics, and swim lessons for all ages. Lifeguard on duty during all open hours.

Puppy Pilates

Get ready to stretch, strengthen, and snuggle at the Philadelphia Culture Center! Our unique Puppy Pilates class offers a fun, all-levels workout that combines gentle Pilates movements with the heartwarming presence of adoptable puppies. Whether you're a seasoned Pilates enthusiast or a beginner looking to try something new, this class welcomes everyone.
Bring your yoga mat and your love for furry friends as you engage in mindful stretches, core strengthening, and balance exercises designed to improve flexibility and reduce stress. Between poses, enjoy playful puppy interactions that will boost your mood and create unforgettable moments of joy and connection.
This event is perfect for animal lovers and wellness seekers alike — plus, all pups are available for adoption through our local rescue partners. Who knows? You might just find your new best friend!

When: June 6, 4pm
Where: Gymnasium

Summer Musical Auditions

Calling all Pink Ladies, T–Birds, and Rydell High hopefuls! The Philadelphia Culture Center is thrilled to announce auditions for our high–energy summer production of Grease, the beloved musical that brings big hair, bigger dance moves, and unforgettable tunes to the stage. Whether you're a seasoned performer or stepping into the spotlight for the first time, we welcome all experience levels to audition.
Please come prepared to sing 16–32 bars of a song in the style of the show (bring sheet music or a backing track), and wear comfortable clothing for a short dance/movement combo. Cold readings from the script will follow. No headshot or résumé required—but feel free to bring them if you have them!

When: June 20, 6pm
Where: PCC Event Hall
Performance Dates: August 14–17
Rehearsals: Weeknights starting June 30 (schedule will be provided at auditions)

Summer Family Field Day

Join us every Saturday afternoon in July for Family Field Day Edition at The Philadelphia Culture Center. This outdoor event brings together families, neighbors, and friends for a playful afternoon of classic field games and friendly competition in our courtyard and gymnasium.
Activities include sack races, spoon-and-egg relays, bean bag toss, water balloon challenges, tug-of-war, and other lawn games designed for all ages. Whether you're in it to win it or just here for the laughs, there's something for everyone — from young children to adults.
We'll have music, light refreshments, and prize ribbons for standout performances and team spirit. This is a great way to unplug, enjoy the outdoors, and spend quality time together in a fun, relaxed environment.

When: Every Saturday in July from 1:00 to 4:00 PM

recent events

PABJ Town Hall

Last week, the Philadelphia Association of Black Journalists (PABJ) held a powerful and engaging town hall at The Philadelphia Culture Center. The event brought together journalists, community leaders, and residents for a dynamic conversation focused on media representation, amplifying community voices, and promoting accountability in local news coverage.
Attendees discussed the challenges and opportunities facing Black journalists and the broader media landscape, exploring ways to foster more inclusive storytelling that truly reflects the diversity of Philadelphia. The town hall provided a valuable platform for open dialogue, networking, and collaboration aimed at strengthening the relationship between media professionals and the communities they serve.

The Philadelphia Culture Center is proud to host events like this that encourage thoughtful discussion and drive positive change.

Community Craft Fair

Last month, the Community Roots Center buzzed with energy during our vibrant Community Craft Fair. Local artists and artisans showcased an array of handmade goods, from unique jewelry and artwork to home decor and specialty crafts. Visitors enjoyed exploring one-of-a-kind treasures while supporting small businesses right in their own neighborhood.
The fair was more than just shopping — it was a celebration of creativity, community spirit, and connection. Attendees mingled, shared stories, and discovered new talents, making the day a memorable experience for all.

We look forward to welcoming even more artists and visitors at future fairs that continue to highlight the rich creativity within our community.

Resume & Interview Workshop

Two weeks ago, the Philadelphia Culture Center hosted a highly successful Resume & Interview Workshop designed to equip job seekers with essential skills to navigate today's competitive job market. Attendees received personalized guidance on crafting compelling resumes and cover letters that stand out to employers, along with practical tips for mastering the interview process.
The interactive session included mock interviews, feedback from career coaches, and strategies for confidently answering common interview questions. Participants left empowered with tools and confidence to take the next step in their career journeys.

We're proud to offer workshops like this that support professional growth and community advancement. Stay tuned for upcoming career development events!
