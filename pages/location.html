how to find us

By Car

From I–95, take Exit 22 toward Center City/Callowhill Street. Follow signs for Vine Street Expressway (I–676 W) and take the Broad Street exit. Turn left onto Broad Street and continue for approximately half a mile. PCC will be directly ahead at the intersection of Broad & Market Streets. Parking is available at nearby garages including the Love Park Garage and The Wanamaker Garage (both within 2–3 blocks).

By Subway

Take the Broad Street Line to City Hall Station or the Market–Frankford Line to 15th Street Station. Follow signage for "City Hall Courtyard" or exit to street level via the northeast corner stairwell.

By Bus

Numerous SEPTA bus routes stop at or near City Hall, including Routes 17, 33, 38, and 44. Use the stop at Broad & Market Streets.

By Bike

Philadelphia City Hall is accessible via protected bike lanes on Spruce, Pine, and 13th Street. Bike racks are available at Dilworth Park, directly adjacent to the west entrance of City Hall.

if you need help finding us, call (215) 555–0140 or visit the information desk at the south entrance.
