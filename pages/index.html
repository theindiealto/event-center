about us
At PCC, we believe community is more than just geography—it's connection, support, and shared growth. Founded in 1994, our center was created as a safe, inclusive space where people of all ages, backgrounds, and abilities can come together to learn, create, celebrate, and thrive.

Whether you're here for a weekly fitness class, a local art showcase, a family event, or just a friendly face, you'll find a welcoming environment that reflects the spirit of our neighborhood. We offer a wide range of programs—from youth and senior services to educational workshops, wellness activities, and cultural events—designed to meet the evolving needs of our community.

Driven by passionate staff, dedicated volunteers, and incredible local partners, PCC is proud to be a home base for connection, creativity, and community-driven change.
